About

As a pharmacist who is presently working in a university teaching hospital in Nigeria. Healthcare professionals often lack access to comprehensive patient data, hindering their ability to deliver quality care. For instance, this is evident when patients visit the pharmacy with only a prescription form, leaving behind their patient folder in the ward. Pharmacists, however, play a broader role than just dispensing medication; they are trained to provide holistic pharmaceutical care, including counseling and medication management. xPatientCare aims to streamline patient data collection, storage, and retrieval, reducing errors and improving accessibility. Also, xPatientCare seeks to solve unauthorized alteration, forgery, or manipulation of prescriptions, enabling illegal obtaining of controlled substances.
